One thing I'd actually move way down
#35 Optimize Center

I know we spent time redesigning it, but think about how people actually use a media library.

They will:

Browse every day.
Search every day.
View photos every day.
Make collections regularly.

They'll probably run Optimize:

Once after importing a big library.
Occasionally after adding new media.

That makes it a maintenance feature rather than a core experience. I'd make the Library feel exceptional first, then add optimization.

So my top three priorities would be:

#47 – Immersive Media Viewer
#48 – Background Thumbnail Generation
#12 – Background Tasks Panel

Once those are done, the Media Center will feel like a polished, professional application instead of just a capable backend. Everything after that builds on a strong user experience.
