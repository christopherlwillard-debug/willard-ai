Overall

If I were prioritizing implementation, I'd actually build them in this order:

#177 (Duplicate Review) — Highest risk because it deletes user data. Getting the review and staging flow right is critical.
#176 (Optimize Center) — Adds intelligent optimization while protecting originals through verification and user choice.

The common thread between both tasks is something I think is worth making a guiding principle for Willard AI:

Never destroy user data without first making the decision transparent, verifiable, and reversible whenever possible.

That philosophy fits the archival-quality application you're building and will make users much more comfortable trusting it with irreplaceable photos and videos.
