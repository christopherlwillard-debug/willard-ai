I think there's an even better next feature

Since you're building Willard AI rather than cloning Immich, I'd make the next roadmap item something like:

#121 – Intelligent Library Watcher

Done looks like:

Watches all configured library folders while the app is running.
New files are indexed within seconds.
Deleted files are removed from the index automatically.
Renamed or moved files update without losing metadata.
Changes are processed in small batches to avoid overloading the NAS.
Falls back to a verification scan if watcher events are missed.

Then users almost never need to think about scanning again.
