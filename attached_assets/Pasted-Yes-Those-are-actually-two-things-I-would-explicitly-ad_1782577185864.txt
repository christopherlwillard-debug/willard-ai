#4 - Content Hash for Duplicate Detection
What & Why
The cleanup/duplicates endpoint groups files by content_hash, but the scan engine (scan.ts) doesn't compute hashes yet — the field is always null, so no duplicates are detected. Adding SHA-256 hashing during scan (for files under ~500MB) enables real duplicate detection.

scan.ts computes SHA-256 hash for each file (streaming, skip files > 500MB)
Hashes stored in indexed_files.content_hash
Cleanup > Duplicates page shows real duplicate groups with wasted space
Relevant files

lib/db/src/schema/files.ts — content_hash field already exists
