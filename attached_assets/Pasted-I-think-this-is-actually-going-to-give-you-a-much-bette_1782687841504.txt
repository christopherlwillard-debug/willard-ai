The important part is:

Your NAS is the master copy.
Your Replit app controls everything.
Immich only indexes and searches.

That means if you ever replace Immich in five years, you don't have to move a single file.

My recommendation

I wouldn't spend any more time trying to make Immich own the NAS storage.

The installation is working. We know the API, AI features, and interface are all functioning.

The next step is to start building the Media Center in Replit and design it so that:
