Overall

I think you've made the right call by stepping away from Docker and Immich for now.

The application you're building is becoming a Digital Asset Manager, not just a photo viewer. If you get the scanner, media database, thumbnails, browser, search, and organization tools right, you'll already have a powerful Media Center.

Then, months down the road, if you decide you want AI features, you can add an "AI Services" layer that plugs into your existing Media Center rather than having the Media Center depend on it. I think that's a much stronger foundation and a better fit for the way you want to use your NAS.
