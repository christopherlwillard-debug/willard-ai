I think this feature is absolutely essential, but I would make it much bigger. This is one of those features that separates a basic file browser from a polished media application.

Here's how I'd spec it.

#47 - Immersive Media Viewer (Photos, Videos & Documents)

What & Why

The Media Center should provide a modern, distraction-free viewing experience for all supported media without requiring downloads or external applications.

Users should be able to browse their entire library using keyboard shortcuts, mouse gestures, or touch controls while keeping their place in the Library.

The viewer should feel similar to Apple Photos, Google Photos, or Lightroom.

One feature I'd add immediately

Since you're already building the map feature, add a Map button right inside the viewer.

Imagine you're looking at a vacation photo.

Instead of digging through menus, you click:

📍 Show on Map

The map opens, centered exactly where that photo was taken.

That ties the viewer directly into your location feature and makes the whole Media Center feel much more integrated.

One last design recommendation

I would avoid traditional pop-up modals.

Instead, make the viewer take over the entire application, similar to Apple Photos or Plex:
