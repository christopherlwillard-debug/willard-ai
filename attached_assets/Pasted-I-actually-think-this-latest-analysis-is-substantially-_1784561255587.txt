Priority: Highest (10/10)

This is the biggest finding in your latest investigation.

I'd make one additional improvement

Don't only save at the end.

Save periodically.

For example:

every 100 directories
every 30 seconds
when the scanner pauses
when it shuts down gracefully

That way the cache is always improving, even if a scan is interrupted.
