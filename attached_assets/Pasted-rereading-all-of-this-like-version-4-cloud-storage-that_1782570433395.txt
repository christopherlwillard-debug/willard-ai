I want it to reorganize and move things around on my NAS will that be possible 

Yes—but I would not make that part of Version 1. I would build the architecture so it's possible from day one, but keep the first release read-only until you trust it.

For your collection, we're talking about 7+ TB of irreplaceable data. One bug in an automatic organizer could move thousands of files into the wrong folders. It's worth being cautious.

Here's the progression I'd recommend:

Version 1: Analyze (Read-only)
Scan the entire NAS.
Find duplicates.
Peek inside ZIP files.
Detect messy folders.
Identify photos that belong in Immich.
Suggest how everything should be organized.
Show exactly what it would do, but don't change anything.
Version 2: Preview & Approve

The AI builds a complete organization plan.

Version 3: Automated Organization

Once you've used it enough to trust it, you could create rules like:

Any new ZIP in Downloads → extract automatically.
Any new photos → send to Immich.
Any scanned receipt → Documents/Receipts.
Any duplicate → move to a Review folder.
Empty folders → remove automatically.

That becomes your personal automation system.
